# Glossary

Version: 1.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Decision Operating System (DOS)

A platform that supports operational decision-making by maintaining an
Operational Picture and producing explainable recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# FO Brain

The first implementation of the Decision Operating System.

Initial domain: Family Office operations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Operational Brief

The aggregate that owns the lifecycle of a single operational case.

It contains the Operational Picture and all related reasoning artifacts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Operational Picture

The platform's current understanding of the operational situation.

It is the basis for all recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Fact

Verified information accepted as true within an Operational Brief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Unknown

Information that is required but not yet available.

Unknowns remain visible until resolved.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Assumption

A working hypothesis used until sufficient evidence is available.

Assumptions are never treated as facts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Constraint

A condition that limits available options.

Examples include budget, time, policy, law and authority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Risk

A potential event that may negatively affect the desired outcome.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Outcome

The desired operational result.

All recommendations are evaluated against the intended outcome.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation

A proposed course of action derived from the Operational Picture.

Recommendations support human decisions.

They are never decisions themselves.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Decision Engine

The reasoning component responsible for analysing operational change and
updating the Operational Picture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Information Intake

The component responsible for receiving, normalising and routing
incoming information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Learning Engine

The component that extracts reusable knowledge from completed
Operational Briefs.

Learning does not modify historical records.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knowledge Repository

The collection of reusable organisational knowledge.

It stores patterns, policies and reasoning assets.

It does not store Operational Brief state.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Timeline

A chronological record of significant operational events.

It explains how understanding evolved over time.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Human Authority

The principle that final operational decisions always belong to
authorised people.

AI may analyse and recommend.

Humans approve and remain accountable.
